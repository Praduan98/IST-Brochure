Buyers leave signals before they fill forms.

Modern B2B buyers don’t move through clean funnels. They research quietly, compare vendors and ask peers — leaving signals long before they ever fill a form.

Most teams already have the data. The problem is it’s scattered across CRM records, ad platforms, website analytics, enrichment tools and sales notes. That’s why traditional GTM feels noisy.

02 — The Problem

Why traditional GTM is breaking.

04

Traditional GTM was built around visible leads. Someone fills a form, downloads a resource, books a demo — then sales reacts. But by then, the buyer has already done most of their thinking: compared competitors, checked reviews, visited pricing, shortlisted vendors.

The old playbook reacts too late

Buyers run most of the journey in the dark — comparing, shortlisting, deciding — long before a form is ever filled.

Framework 03 — BRIDGE Ads

Ads that activate existing intent.

07

Most B2B ads still target cold audiences with generic messaging, optimising for clicks or impressions. BRIDGE Ads work differently: they don’t create demand from scratch — they activate demand that already exists, warming signal-qualified accounts before, during and after sales engagement.

How BRIDGE Ads work

One buyer signal, five connected moves — warming a cold, anonymous trigger into a booked conversation.
